## Top-risk acceptance criteria

R1 is inherent and can only be reduced, never closed. V1 accepts it only with explicit language, health/gap visibility, two partner workflows, and a missed-change correction path. R8 is also structurally expected; honest loss of coverage is safer than circumvention. R10–R12 determine whether the service is sustainable: if no backup reviewer, standing partner use, and funding path exist at the release decision, hold or sunset rather than label an unattended prototype “V1.0.”
