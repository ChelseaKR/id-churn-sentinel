# Commercial status

**Effective date:** July 14, 2026  
**Status:** Commercial activity paused

ID Churn Sentinel may continue as public-interest research, safety analysis,
documentation, and open-source technical work. Nothing in this repository is a
current offer for sale, rate card, solicitation, sponsorship request, pilot
invitation, statement of customer adoption, or representation that revenue is
being earned.

While this hold is active, do not use repository materials to:

- contact or recruit prospective customers, partners, funders, or pilot sites;
- quote prices, propose paid work, accept payment, or negotiate a contract;
- claim a partner, customer, subscription, integration, pilot, sale, or revenue;
- activate a commercial service, paid support channel, or sales funnel; or
- describe roadmap targets as completed or currently underway business activity.

The pricing ranges, segment descriptions, outreach sequence, funnel targets, and
paid-service concepts in [`11-GTM-BUSINESS-MODEL.md`](./11-GTM-BUSINESS-MODEL.md)
are historical hypotheses preserved for provenance. They are not approved
future actions and should not be relied on as evidence of willingness to pay.

Ending the hold requires a new, dated written status update after ownership,
outside-activity, privacy, safety, operating-capacity, and authorization reviews.
Removing this notice alone does not end the hold.
